Key decisions locked in the plan

Placement logic

Seed = clamp(age − 6, K, 6) — one rung below age-expected. Pass = at most one miss per set. Walk up on pass, down on fail; first-failed rung is the recommendation. Fail K → Level K; pass 7 → Geometry/Algebra II note. Tests prove the result is independent of the entry age.

Child experience

No red X's, no scores, no timer; "I don't know yet" is a silent miss. Checkpoint K is parent-assisted (read-aloud + yes/no checks). Numeric answers accept commas and spaces.

Editability

Everything the curriculum author might change — problem wording, answers, skill text, product URLs — lives in js/data.js, guarded by a validation test suite.
